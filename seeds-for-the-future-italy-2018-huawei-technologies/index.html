Seeds for the Future Italy 2018 @ Huawei Technologies

Published by on

Seeds for the Future Italy 2018: 2-week study trip to China for 10 Master students selected from top Italian ICT universities. Participants gained first-hand work experience and insights into the work of a multinational ICT company at Huawei’s global headquarters in Shenzhen as well as training on wireless and core network technologies, 5G solutions, Cloud Computing and the Internet of Things.

Chinese Language & Culture for beginners: Beginner Mandarin Chinese course (pronunciation and pinyin romanization system, elementary vocabulary, basic sentence patterns), Chinese calligraphy and painting at the Beijing Language and Culture University.
